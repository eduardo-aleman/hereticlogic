## What is this?

A personal reading project. The books here span political philosophy, economic history, classical antiquity, urban theory, and the long history of doubt. The reviews focus on one thing: **the quality of the argument**. Not the author's reputation, not the prevailing consensus, not whether the conclusions are comfortable.

The title is a disposition, not a programme. Logic applied without deference to received authority tends to produce heresy. That seems like a reasonable starting point.
